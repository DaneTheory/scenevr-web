m Metaverse.sh

Explore a virtual world in your browser

Metaverse.sh is a virtual world that you can explore in your browser. If you are a 3D artist, you can drag and drop your models into the world for everyone to view.

WebGL based

Metaverse.sh uses WebGL, and supports Firefox and Chrome as browsers.

Free to play

You don't have to sign up or pay anything to use the metaverse. You will need to register an account if you want to upload content.

Supported formats

You can upload models to metaverse using any industry standard tools. We currently only support .fbx, .3ds, .dae and .obj models, but more support is coming soon. Collada (.dae) tends to work best.
